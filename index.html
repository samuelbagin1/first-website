Photography

Photography

I was introduced to photography in early 2020. I loved to take photos so I bought my first drone. I enjoyed taking photos but in photography are some important things:

1. Story telling - the main purpose of taking photos
2. Editing - telling story with your colors

I sucked at this things. So I started my journey DISCOVER UNDISCOVERED.

DISCOVER UNDISCOVERED

DISCOVER UNDISCOVERED

Everyone loves to travel. But do you know your nearby country? DISCOVER UNDISCOVERED is my journey and it can be yours also. It is about trying new things and put you out from your comfort zone. It is easy to shoot beautiful places like Iceland. But try out to shoot normal places and make it awesome using different angles.

I sucked at shooting and editing. I want to be better so I challenged myself to post every day on Instagram for a year. I started on the new year of 2021. And after a month I was starting seeing some results. I was developing my own color pallet, finding out new features and tools about Lightroom, I was starting understand color grading and color correction not just in photo, but in video as well.
